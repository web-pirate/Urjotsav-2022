About Urjotsav 2k22

Urjotsav is for technical as well as cultural fest. In fact, the college atmosphere takes a festive mood on that occasion. Most of the students take part in the College Fest. They enjoy it very much. Often students of other colleges are invited to take an active part in the festival it build team mangament in students Urjotsav - as its name suggests something that is invigorating makes you feel refreshed and more energetic. We @ PIEMR celebrate this festival of amusement and enthusiasm every year as our annual fest. It's a 6 days event where everyone gets restored with power and gets fulfilled with new strength to continue our studies with high stamina.
